Mitipi

Mitipi is a smart insurance company. They provide technological ways to prevent burglaries and offer customizable insurance packages.

The logo is based on the shape of the main product, Kevin. This smart hub can simulate people’s presence with light and sound.

The mark also looks like an object under the light source.

The main color is yellow. The color of warm and cozy light. The color of windows that scare burglars off. The color that confronts all those traditional insurance companies painted blue.
