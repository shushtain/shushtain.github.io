KharkivPride

KharkivPride is an annual LGBT+ Pride event in Kharkiv, Ukraine. It focuses on visibility and counteracting stigma.

Kharkiv is a city of concrete buildings, so the metaphor of the identity is about rainbow beams that crash concrete walls, challenge outdated norms and traditions that work against LGBT+ community.
